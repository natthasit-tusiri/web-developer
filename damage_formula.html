สูตรคำนวณดาเมจ

Actual DMG = พลังโจมตีจริง * Skill Multiplier% * CDMG% * Elemental DMG % * (Skill DMG% + DMG during Resonance%) * DMG Bonus% * * Shield Breaking DMG% * DMG to Debuff% (Different debuff are multiplied separately) * DMG to Enemy Type + Additional DMG (Different element additional DMG are added separately)

พลังโจมตี

PATK shown in Basic Attributes is already calculated in total.
Every 10 Strength increase 1% "STR Increase %".

Total PATK = PATK * (1 + STR Increase %) * (1 + PATK%)

For example, if player have PATK +100, 1000 Strength, PATK +10%, the PATK will be 100 * (1 + 100%) * (1 + 10%), resulting in 220 Total PATK.

Total PATK can be boosted from various sources that states Strength +X, PATK +X%, PATK +X.

ลดการโจมตี

Damage Reduction % is calculated from following formula :
Damage Reduction % = Defense / (3000 + Defense)
Where Defense = PDEF * (1 - Physical PEN%)
For example, if player have 3385 PDEF, and got penetrated by 3% Physical PEN, the calculation is (3385 * (1-0.03)) / (3000 + (3385 * (1-0.03))), resulting in 52.2% Physical DMG Reduction.

พลังโจมตีจริง

Actual Attack is calculated from following formula :
Actual Attack = Total PATK * (1-Damage Reduction %) + PDEF Shred

For example, if player have Total PATK of 1000, 2000 PDEF Shred, enemies have 50% DR, the calculation is 1000 * (1 - 50%) + 2000, resulting in 2500 Actual Attack.

Note: Training Ground have 0 Defense, resulting in 0% DR.
Training Dummy is counted as BOSS unit.

ตัวคูณสกิล

Skill Multiplier is taken from the Skill Page.
This value increase with level, which can be increased from various Skill +1 sources.

คริติคอลดาเมจ

Critical Damage occur when landing a critical hit, increasing the damage multiplicatively.
To make use of this stats, a good critical rate is needed.
Base Critical DMG is 50%, which means 150% of normal DMG.
Critical DMG can be gained through various sources that states CDMG +X%.

ดาเมจธาตู

Elemental DMG is affected by Elemental ENH points, with ratio of 11 attribute equal to 5% Elemental DMG.
Elemental DMG can be gained through various sources that states Ele ENH +X.

Ele Res substracts directly from Ele ENH.
Leading us to formula of Ele DMG (%) = (Ele ENH - Ele RES)/2.2.

สกิลดาเมจ

Skill DMG can be gained through various sources that states Skill DMG +X%.
Class effect that boost DMG of certain skills also falls into this category.

ดาเมจโบนัส

DMG Bonus can be gained through various sources that states DMG Bonus +X%.

ดาเมจประสานพลัง

Activating Resonance boost Damage by 20% at minimum and counted additively to Skill DMG and BATK, and can be boosted from various sources that states "DMG During Resonance +X%"

ดาเมจเกราะแตก

Breaking Shield boost Damage by 30% at minimum and counted multiplicatively, and can be boosted from various sources that states "DMG During Shield Break +X%"

ดาเมจดีบัฟ

Dealing DMG to debuffed enemies (scorched, vulnerable, slowed, exhausted) are counted separately and multiplicatively.
Below is the formula for it : (1 +DMG to Scorched) * (1 +DMG to Vulnerable) * (1 + DMG to Slowed) * (DMG to Exhausted)
